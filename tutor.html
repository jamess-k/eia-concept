Session Outline

Open Hours was created to give students on our waiting list a way start learning English right away. Open Hours is a casual drop-in class, currently offered on Zoom every Thursday from 6 to 7:30 p.m. Students and tutors form small groups and practice their English by having conversations, reading from books and newspapers, asking questions and playing games. Volunteer tutors in this program include both advanced students and native English speakers.

Source: English in Action

Typical Session

This is what a typical tutoring session may look like.

  • 6:00pm | Join the Zoom
  • 6:00 - 6:15pm | Introductions and Creating Breakout Rooms
  • 6:15 - 6:30pm | Meet your Group for the Day
  • 6:30pm | Check in from Angela or other Supervisor
  • 6:30 - 7:15pm | Tutoring
  • Review Topics at the Student's Request
  • Introduce New Matieral (Vocabulary, Pronunciation, etc.)
  • Conversation and Practice in English
  • 7:15pm | Return to Main Room
  • 7:15pm - 7:30pm | Takeaways and Final Discussion
